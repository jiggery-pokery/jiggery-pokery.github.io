Christmas eCard

Concept & Art Direction, Illustration, Design and front-end development

When we think of Christmas, it often conjures up images and sounds of the church choir, and a sense of peace and tranquility. I wanted to impart those feelings to the user together with the messages from the company.

I decided on the use of a cinemagraph, by which the juxtaposition of static and moving objects help to draw the attention of the user into the visuals. A scene of a winter’s night was chosen to set the mood.
